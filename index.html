Purpose

The core values of DIVD.fund are integrity, connectivity and empowerment, where DIVD.fund is a dependable, independent and respected partner.

Mission

DIVD.fund stands for a healthy, secure and robust internet for everyone. By stimulating and financing innovative ideas, concepts and projects, DIVD.fund enhances security in the digital world. Within a safe climate and a stimulating and encouraging environment, young adults can develop their knowledge, skills and experience. We are inclusive and have an open mindset. We are impartial and apolitical. Trust is our greatest asset.

Vision

DIVD.fund believes in the power of talent, the quality of the individual, and in the combined strength of these individuals working together. Based on a shared mindset and intrinsic drive to make the internet a safer place, DIVD.fund brings together personal growth and joint development. People, companies and organisations join us on our mission to connect innovation, knowledge, capital and talent. The resulting synergy implies no ambition is too great, and no goal is unachievable, which strengthens the individual, the community and society.

Strategy

To achieve its mission and vision in the first five years of its existence (2023 - 2027), DIVD.fund has five strategic goals that are crucial for its success.

Projects

DIVD.fund helps launch projects as a cybersecurity innovation incubator to support startups, educational programs for young adults in cybersecurity, research collaborations on emerging technologies, awareness campaigns for the public, and partnerships with other organizations to collectively address cybersecurity challenges. These initiatives align with DIVD.fund's mission of promoting digital security and creating a safer online environment.
